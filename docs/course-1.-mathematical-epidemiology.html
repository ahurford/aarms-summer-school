Course 1. Mathematical Epidemiology

This course will cover different formulations and analyses for mathematical models in epidemiology. Topics include host heterogeneity, multiple pathogens, seasonality, spatial spread, within-host dynamics, and zoonotic spillover. Some lectures will be based on Modelling Infectious Diseases in Humans and Animals by Matt Keeling and Pejman Rohani (available as .pdf from MUN library).
